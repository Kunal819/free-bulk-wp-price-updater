== Description ==
Free Bulk WP Price Updatere is a free plugin designed to help you easily update product prices for both variable and simple products in your WooCommerce store. This plugin provides different formulas to increment, decrement, multiply, or divide product prices in bulk.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I use the plugin? =
After activating the plugin, navigate to the "Bulk Price Editor". Select the field according to your requirement.

= What formulas are available? =
- Increment: Increase the product price by a fixed amount or percentage.
- Decrement: Decrease the product price by a fixed amount or percentage.
- Multiply: Multiply the product price by a specific factor.
- Divide: Divide the product price by a specific factor.

= Can I update prices for both variable and simple products? =
Yes, this plugin allows you to update prices for both variable and simple products in your WooCommerce store.

= Is this plugin free? =
Yes, Free Bulk WP Price Updater is completely free to use.
